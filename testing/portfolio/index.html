VirtualDissent_hyper.txt

HTML, CSS, JavaScript | May 2020

VirtualDissent_hyper.txt is made in collaboration with Maria Paula Calderon, Neyva Hernandez, and William Mlekush. The project is a virual exhibition of hyper-text art curated by us. Each art piece exhibited has its own curatorial page which describes the piece. The design of the curatorial pages is directly inspired by the landing page of the piece exhibited, as to create the feel of an exhibition room.
